Welcome to DIA-CoE IIT Kharagpur

DRDO Industry Academia Centre of Excellence (DIA-CoE) was established by DRDO at premier and capable academic Institutes and Universities in the country to build Directed Research Ecosystem through collaboration among DRDO labs, Academia and Startups/Industries. DIA-CoEs will pursue research both in developing cutting-edge technologies and in the areas of denied defence technologies.

The primary aim of establishing such DIA-CoEs is to harness & synergize the combined strength of academia, student community, research fellows, niche technology industries & DRDO scientist to provide research & innovations in identified futuristic defence technological domains. These DIA-CoEs will boost & sustain development of advanced technologies for future defence systems and platforms through multi-disciplinary & multi-institutional collaborative efforts. DRDO has established 15 Nos. of DIA-CoEs so far.

DIA-CoE started in the year 2020 at IIT Kharagpur with implementation of research areas on defence applications and developing cutting edge technologies through 4 major research verticals. The DIA- CoE is spread over 48000 sq ft. at IIT Kharagpur.
