Welcome to the DIA-CoE IIT Kharagpur

The DRDO Industry Academia Centre of Excellence (DIA-CoE) at IIT Kharagpur is a hub of innovation and collaborative research in defense technologies.

DIA-CoEs are established by DRDO in premier academic institutions to foster a research ecosystem with academia, industry, and startups. Our center is one of 15 across India, dedicated to developing cutting-edge and critical defense technologies.

Since its inception in 2020, DIA-CoE IIT Kharagpur has operated across 48,000 sq. ft., focusing on four major research verticals and collaborating with top scientists, students, and innovators.
