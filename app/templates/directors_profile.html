Dr. Dipak Kumar Das

Director, DIA CoE IIT Kharagpur

About the Director

Dr Dipak Kumar Das took over as Director, DIA-CoE IIT Kharagpur in January 2024. Prior to that, he served as Scientist H at the Defence Metallurgical Research Laboratory, Hyderabad, and held the position of Associate Director there for 13 years.

He earned his B.Tech and M.Tech in Metallurgical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, and his PhD from IIT BHU. He conducted Post-Doctoral research in laser-induced breakdown spectroscopy (LIBS) on coatings and Ni-base superalloy substrates.

With over 30 years of experience in Surface Engineering and Vacuum Investment Casting, Dr Das has contributed to protective coatings for gas turbines, Ti-alloys, oxidation-resistant coatings, and superalloy development.

He has executed several major projects at DMRL, Hyderabad, and is hands-on with advanced systems such as plasma sprays, oxidation rigs, CVD units, vacuum furnaces, lasers, SEMs, and EPMA.

Dr Das has published over 100 papers, delivered keynote talks, and continues pioneering research in coatings for hypersonic applications and novel casting techniques.
